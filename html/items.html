Contributing to a kill grants the holder +20 Attack Damage for the remainder of combat. This effect can stack any number of times (starting at 1).
Prevents the wearer's first death, placing them in stasis instead. After 2 seconds, they return with 400 Health and shed all negative effects.
When combat begins, the wearer and all allies within 1 hexes in the same row gain 35% bonus Attack Speed for the rest of combat.
The holder's spells heal them for 33% of the damage dealt. Excess healing fuels a shield that protects the holder against up to 400 damage
Basic Attacks heal the holder for 40% of the damage dealt.
The holder's spells and basic attacks do 10% bonus damage. If the target has more than 1750 maximum Health, the bonus increases to 90%.
[Unique] Grants 75% Critical Strike Chance (including components). Each point of Critical Strike Chance above 100% become +1% Critical Strike Damage.
[Unique] The holder gains the Divine trait.
The holder's basic attacks restore 5 Mana on-hit.
